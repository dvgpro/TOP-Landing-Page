Penguins are awesome

Penguins are weird, cute flightless birds. Mainly living in Antartica, they hunt and live in the World's harshest climate.

Some random information

These brown penguins still have their baby feathers.

These penguins walk from their nests to the ocean to hunt for food.

These curious creatures don't mind close human contact

Penguins are amazing swimmers, hunting for fish deep under the ocean.
